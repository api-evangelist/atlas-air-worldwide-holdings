Brought together by Atlas Air Worldwide, signed helmets from Aston Martin Aramco Formula One driver Lance Stroll and Miami Dolphins NFL player Jordan Phillips were auctioned to support The Movember Foundation. The final donation includes the winning auction bid plus matching contributions from Atlas and Aston Martin Aramco Formula One Team. Prior to the sale through F1 Authentics and Momento, the two athletes met at the 2026 Miami Grand Prix to exchange the helmets.
